Attendance Record Template

This simple spreadsheet is a great way to record attendance at meetings, clubs, scouts, church, and Sunday School classes. Print the blank attendance record for your notebook or clipboard. Then transfer the information to your spreadsheet to keep an electronic attendance record. You can enter arbitrary dates for tracking meeting attendance, or if you are tracking church attendance or Sunday school attendance, you can enter the first date in cell C5 manually and then use a formula like =C5+7 for the following week. Simply copy/insert/paste existing rows or columns to add additional people or dates. Attendance is totaled by date and by person. Updated 4/4/2014 : We updated the XLSX version to make it easy to edit with Excel for iPad.

Dashboard HR

📊 The HR Dashboard in Excel is a visual tool that effectively consolidates, analyzes, and presents human resources data. It serves as an “information hub” that enables HR departments and business leaders to make quick and accurate decisions.

🎯 Main Purpose: To consolidate HR data from various sources, including personal information, compensation, attendance, training, and performance evaluations. Visualize the information using charts, data tables, and KPI indicators for easier tracking. Support decision-making by helping managers identify trends, detect issues, and provide timely solutions.

✅ Benefits of Using the HR Dashboard in Excel

  • Time-saving: No need to manually process individual data tables.

  • Easy customization: Excel allows you to design dashboards tailored to each business’s specific needs

  • Enhanced transparency: All information is clearly presented and easy to review.>
